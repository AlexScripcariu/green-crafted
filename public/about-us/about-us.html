FAQ

  1. How long do the orders take?

    Depending on the item, and the quantity of it, the order could take a couple of days to weeks. We do not have larger products on demand therefore there will small wait to aquire them. I hope that is understandable and if there are any inconveniences we do apologise on our behalf.

  2. What is your return policy?

    We offer a 60-day full refund policy for any products that do not meet your expectations. Simply contact our support team, and they will guide you through the return process. Please note that items must be returned in their original condition.

  3. Can I track my order?

    Yes, once your order is shipped, you will receive an email with a tracking link. You can use this link to monitor the status and expected delivery date of your package.

  4. Do you offer discounts or promotions?

    Yes, we frequently run seasonal promotions and offer discounts for first-time buyers. Sign up for our newsletter to stay updated on the latest deals and exclusive offers.

  5. Are your products handmade?

    Many of our products are handmade by skilled artisans who use sustainable techniques passed down through generations. This ensures each item is unique, high-quality, and crafted with care for both you and the environment.

  6. What makes Green Crafted products eco-friendly?

    At Green Crafted, we prioritize sustainability by using natural, biodegradable, and recycled materials in our products. We collaborate with local artisans, ensure fair trade practices, and utilize minimal, recyclable packaging. Every step of our process is designed to minimize environmental impact while delivering high-quality, durable goods.
